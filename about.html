For as long as I can remember I've had a love for the outdoors. Growing up my family spent a lot of time at a family lake in Northern Minnesota and as soon as I was able, I went West to Montana. I attended Montana State in Bozeman and have stuck around ever since. I'm very fortunate to have been able to live in a place that has virtually everything I love to do; including camping, hiking, mtb biking, rafting and skiing.

My more recent passion has been focused on technology and programming. I made the choice to go back to school in 2016 for a Bachelor's in Computer Science. While hard and time-consuming I have found that overall I enjoy it immensely.
